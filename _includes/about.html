About

I am Gerald Balekaki, an Assistant Teaching Professor of Computer Science at Illinois Institute of Technology. My research focuses on Computational Science, Databases, High-Performance Computing, and Computer Networks.

I have a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Cape Town, where I worked on scientific database modeling for Computational Radio Astronomy. I am an active member of ACM, IEEE, IITPSA, ISOC, and several technical committees.

You can explore my publications and projects here. Feel free to reach out to collaborate or discuss technology and research in computing.
